🎓
Our Story

A campus problem,
solved by students

Every day at PUP Sta. Mesa, thousands of students face the same struggle: long queues at the Lagoon, limited break time, and the impossibility of getting food between classes without being late.

IskoDash was born from this frustration — a hyper-localized, peer-to-peer food delivery platform designed exclusively for the PUP campus environment.

Our Story

A campus problem,
solved by students

Every day at PUP Sta. Mesa, thousands of students face the same struggle: long queues at the Lagoon, limited break time, and the impossibility of getting food between classes without being late.

IskoDash was born from this frustration — a hyper-localized, peer-to-peer food delivery platform designed exclusively for the PUP campus environment.
